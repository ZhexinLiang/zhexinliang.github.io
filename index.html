About Me

I am currently a research assistant in MMLab@NTU and I am looking for a Ph.D. position to enroll in during 2024. I obtained M.S. degree in Artifical Intelligence in 2023 from the school of computer science and engineering at Nanyang Technological University, advised by Prof. Chen Change Loy and Prof. Chongyi Li. I obtained B.S. degree in Information Engineering from Zhejiang University in 2021.

I am interested in computer vision and neuroscience. My current work focuses on image enhancement and prompt learning. In the past, I have focused my reseach topics on reinforcement learning, DNN model compression and wearable embedded system for the early screening of intracranial hemorrhage (ICH).
